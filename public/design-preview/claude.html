Claude

A warm-canvas editorial interface for Anthropic's Claude product. Anchored on a tinted cream canvas with serif display headlines, warm coral CTAs, and dark navy product surfaces. The cream + coral pairing is the brand voltage — deliberately warm and humanist where most AI brands use cool blue + slate.
